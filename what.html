It's a cóisir!

Time to reunite and celebrate the Walsh teaghlach

Where: Juneau, Alaska

When: July 1 - 5, 2022

Who: all those related to the Walsh Family from Ballinadee, County Cork, Ireland

July 2

Outdoor Beach Barbecue at Auke Rec Picnic Site

Two buses are reserved for a 4:30pm pick-up to Auke Bay, with a 9:30pm return. We have the shelter all day and until midnight so people can go any time and stay later if they have alternate transportation. For the first bus, we have three pick-up locations. 1) Downtown Douglas, 2) Breeze In, by the Juneau-Douglas Bridge 3) Centennial Hall in the Center of town.

The second buss is for the Valley or out by the glacier. We need to know if people are interested in a bus ride from the Valley to Auke Bay. And we need the addresses of where you’re staying. Each bus can carry 50 people.

If there is not enough interest, we’ll cancel one bus.

The cost will be approximately $20.00/per person round trip. I priced a Lyft from Douglas to Auke Bay and the quotes are $46-60 one-way. There is parking for anyone who wants to drive.

July 3

10:00am to 1:00pm Come explore and learn about the rich Walsh history in Ireland and Alaska at the Treadwell Historical Preservation & Restoration Society. For anyone interested, we’ll meet at 9:00am to run, hike or explore the trail. This event is for anyone who is interested in hearing family history, sharing photos, and viewing the family tree. This is an outdoor event so dress according to the weather.

The rest of the day is open for exploring, shopping, and relaxing. Don't forget about the fireworks after midnight!

July 4

Parades! Soap box derby! Block party at John & Dawn Walsh's place!
